Shabicon SiteManager

To digitize construction management workflows, AC SHABICON develops and publishes the proprietary B2B application Shabicon SiteManager.

Our published software allows site engineers, project managers, and specialty trade contractors to securely review architectural blueprints, monitor daily construction progress, and manage safety compliance reports directly from their Android devices on the job site.

  • Digital Blueprint Viewer: Access CAD files and structural designs on-site.
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor construction milestones and project timelines.
  • Safety & Compliance: Instant submission of site inspection reports.
  • Resource Management: Track heavy machinery and materials logistics.
